Types of Milling Operations

Milling operations are used for machining various surfaces and performing a wide range of processes:

  • Machining flat surfaces;
  • Cutting slots and grooves;
  • Producing shoulders;
  • Forming contoured surfaces;
  • Thread cutting;
  • Manufacturing gears.

Different types of milling cutters are used to perform these operations:

  1. Cylindrical — for machining flat surfaces;
  2. Face — for milling planes and shoulders;
  3. Slitting — for cutting slots and separating metal;
  4. End — a versatile type for various operations;
  5. Angle — for machining inclined surfaces;
  6. Form — for creating complex-shaped surfaces.

The selection of a milling cutter type directly depends on the nature of the operation and the required machining accuracy.
